Get Involved

Three ways to make a difference for Tampa Bay. Every one of them matters, and none of them require experience.

01 — Free
Track 01

Volunteer

Show up, dig in, and leave the beach better than you found it. No experience needed — we supply the gloves, bags, and guidance. You bring the energy.

  • Free gloves, trash bags, and equipment at every event
  • All-levels welcome — families, students, retirees
  • Digital cleanup certificate for your records
  • Community cookout at quarterly mega-events
Sign Up Free →
02 — Any Amount
Track 02

Donate

Not everyone can make it to the beach on a Saturday morning — but your dollars can. Every contribution funds cleanup supplies, site permits, and education programs.

  • $25 funds gloves and bags for 10 volunteers
  • $50 covers a site permit for one cleanup event
  • $100 sponsors a school education session
  • Quarterly impact report sent to all donors
Donate Now →
03 — Orgs & Businesses
Track 03

Corporate Partner

Align your organization with environmental action that your employees and customers care about. We coordinate volunteer days, co-branding, and impact reporting for partners of all sizes.

  • Private cleanup events for your team (25–200 people)
  • Co-branded event photos and social content
  • Partner logo on our website and event materials
  • Custom impact report with data on pounds collected
Get in Touch →
