The People Behind the Work

Our Team

CoastalCleans is powered by a small founding team and a growing family of dedicated volunteers who love Tampa Bay.

Founders & Leadership

The founding team

Three Tampa Bay locals who decided the beach wasn't going to clean itself.

Maria Santos
Executive Director & Co-Founder

Marine biologist and lifelong Tampa Bay resident. Maria founded CoastalCleans after years of watching plastic accumulate on local shorelines where she once played as a child.

David Chen
Operations Director & Co-Founder

Former logistics manager who brought organizational discipline to what started as informal beach meetups. David turns big cleanup visions into smooth, well-supplied events.

Priya Nair
Community & Partnerships

Communications specialist and community organizer. Priya built our volunteer network from the ground up and manages relationships with corporate and school partners across Tampa Bay.
