Our Team

CoastalCleans is powered by a founding team of four and a growing family of dedicated volunteers who love Tampa Bay.

Founders & Leadership

The Founding Team

Four people who decided Tampa Bay's coastlines deserved better — and built the organization to do something about it.

Elizabeth Chojnacki
Chief Executive Officer & Co-Founder

Leads the organization's strategy, partnerships, and long-term vision for expanding coastal conservation across Tampa Bay and beyond.

Yuktha Korlakunta
Chief Financial Officer & Co-Founder

Manages CoastalCleans' finances, donor relations, and grant applications — making sure every dollar reaches the beach.

Leandro Plouz
Chief Technical Officer

Builds and maintains CoastalCleans' digital infrastructure — from the website to volunteer data systems — keeping operations running efficiently.

Madison Lee
Chief Design Officer

Shapes how CoastalCleans looks and feels — from event branding to community communications that inspire people to show up and take action.
