Who We Are

A community built
around clean water

CoastalCleans is a volunteer-run nonprofit dedicated to protecting Tampa Bay's coastlines. We believe that clean beaches are everyone's responsibility — and everyone's reward. From weekend cleanups to corporate partnerships, every action removes plastic before it becomes ocean pollution.
